Plantation Overview

Our raw materials are sourced directly from our privately-owned bamboo plantations in Hunan and Guangdong provinces, covering a total area of 20 square kilometers. These lush bamboo forests are the foundation of our sustainable business model.

With a fully self-sufficient and vertically integrated supply chain—from raw bamboo to finished products—we maintain complete control over quality, sustainability, and efficiency throughout the entire production process.

Our bamboo plantations are strategically located in regions with optimal growing conditions, ensuring the highest quality raw materials for our products while supporting local economies.

Supporting Local Communities

Our integrated system reinforces the long-term sustainability of the bamboo industry while providing stable employment for thousands of rural workers in Hunan and Guangdong provinces.

We prioritize fair wages, safe working conditions, and professional development opportunities for all employees, many of whom have been with us for generations.

By creating a sustainable economic model centered around bamboo cultivation and processing, we're helping to revitalize rural communities while producing eco-friendly products for global markets.

Our Sustainability Commitment

All bamboo is responsibly harvested from FSC® (FSC-C110780) and CFCC dual-certified forests, ensuring a strong balance between ecological preservation and economic growth.

Our bamboo plantations serve as carbon sinks, absorbing approximately 35% more CO₂ than equivalent hardwood forests, contributing significantly to climate change mitigation efforts.

We practice selective harvesting techniques that maintain the health and vitality of our bamboo groves. Unlike traditional logging, bamboo harvesting doesn't require replanting as new shoots naturally emerge from the existing root system.

Our plantations are managed without chemical pesticides or fertilizers, protecting local water sources and promoting biodiversity throughout the ecosystem.
