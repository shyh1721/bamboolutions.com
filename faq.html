Product Information

What materials are used in your bamboo fiber products?

Our bamboo fiber products are made from 95% natural bamboo fiber and 5% starch binding agents. The bamboo is sourced from our own sustainably managed plantations, ensuring complete traceability and quality control. We use no petroleum-based plastics, melamine, or harmful chemicals in our production process.

The binding agents we use are food-safe and meet all international safety standards including FDA, LFGB, and EU regulations for food contact materials.

What are the temperature limitations of your products?

Our bamboo fiber products are designed to withstand temperatures ranging from -20°C to +120°C, making them suitable for:

  • Freezer storage (-20°C)
  • Refrigeration (2-8°C)
  • Room temperature service
  • Hot food service (up to 100°C)
  • Microwave heating (up to 120°C for up to 3 minutes)

Please note that our products are not suitable for conventional oven use or for heating above 120°C, as this may affect their structural integrity.

What certifications do your products have?

Our bamboo fiber products have obtained numerous international certifications, including:

  • BPI Compostable Certification - Verifying our products meet ASTM D6400 standards for compostability
  • OK Compost Industrial & Home - European certification for industrial and home compostability
  • FDA Approval - Meeting US Food and Drug Administration standards for food contact
  • LFGB Certification - German food safety standard
  • ISO 9001 - Quality management system certification
  • ISO 14001 - Environmental management system certification
  • FSC Certification - Forest Stewardship Council certification for our bamboo sourcing
  • SGS Testing - Independent verification of product quality and safety

We can provide copies of these certifications upon request for your due diligence process.

Are your products microwave and dishwasher safe?

Microwave: Yes, our products are microwave safe for reheating food for up to 3 minutes at standard power settings. They can withstand temperatures up to 120°C.

Dishwasher: While our products can technically withstand dishwasher cleaning, we recommend hand washing for prolonged product life. If using a dishwasher, place items on the top rack and use eco-friendly detergents. Please note that repeated dishwasher use may gradually affect the appearance of the products over time.

For optimal product longevity, we recommend gentle hand washing with mild soap and warm water.

How long do your products last with regular use?

Our bamboo fiber products are designed for single-use or limited reuse applications, depending on the specific product line:

  • Single-use takeaway containers: Designed for one-time use but can be reused several times with proper care
  • Reusable tableware: Can be used up to 50-100 times with proper care and gentle washing

Factors that affect longevity include:

  • Washing method (hand washing extends life)
  • Temperature exposure (extreme temperatures may reduce lifespan)
  • Type of food (highly acidic or oily foods may affect the material over time)
  • Storage conditions (store in dry, room temperature environments)

Unlike plastic products that persist for hundreds of years, our products will naturally begin to break down after extended use, which is part of their eco-friendly design.

Sustainability

How biodegradable are your products?

Our bamboo fiber products are fully biodegradable and compostable, with the following breakdown timeframes:

  • Industrial composting: 90 days (certified to EN 13432 standard)
  • Home composting: 180 days (certified to OK Compost Home standard)
  • Natural environment: 1-2 years (varies based on environmental conditions)

This is in stark contrast to conventional plastic products, which can take 500+ years to break down. Our products break down into carbon dioxide, water, and biomass without leaving harmful microplastics or toxic residues.

The biodegradation process is influenced by factors such as temperature, humidity, microbial activity, and exposure to elements. For optimal composting results, we recommend industrial composting facilities where available.

How does bamboo compare to other materials environmentally?

Bamboo offers significant environmental advantages over other materials used in food packaging:

  • Compared to plastic: Bamboo is renewable, biodegradable, and produces 35% more oxygen and absorbs 40% more carbon dioxide than equivalent tree masses. Unlike plastic, it doesn't release microplastics or persist in the environment for centuries.
  • Compared to paper/cardboard: Bamboo grows 20-30 times faster than trees, requires no replanting after harvesting, uses less water, and doesn't need pesticides or fertilizers. Our bamboo fiber products are also more durable and water-resistant than paper alternatives.
  • Compared to bagasse (sugarcane): While both are agricultural byproducts, bamboo has a higher yield per acre, requires less processing, and has superior structural properties allowing for thinner, stronger products.
  • Compared to PLA (corn-based bioplastic): Bamboo requires less agricultural input, doesn't compete with food crops, and biodegrades more reliably in various conditions without requiring specialized industrial composting facilities.

Our lifecycle assessments show that bamboo fiber products have a 70-90% lower carbon footprint compared to equivalent plastic products when accounting for production, transportation, and end-of-life considerations.

How should your products be disposed of?

For optimal environmental impact, we recommend the following disposal methods in order of preference:

  1. Industrial composting: The fastest decomposition method. Many municipalities now offer commercial composting services.
  2. Home composting: Our products are certified for home composting. Simply add them to your compost bin or pile.
  3. Organic waste collection: In regions with organic waste collection, our products can typically be included (check local guidelines).
  4. General waste: If composting options aren't available, our products will still biodegrade faster than conventional alternatives in landfill conditions, though the process is slower.

Important disposal notes:

  • Remove any non-compostable stickers or labels before composting
  • Food residue doesn't need to be removed before composting
  • Cut or break larger items into smaller pieces to speed up the composting process
  • Our products should NOT be placed in recycling bins for plastic, paper, or glass

What sustainable practices do you employ in your manufacturing?

Sustainability is at the core of our entire operation, from raw material sourcing to manufacturing:

  • Renewable energy: Our production facilities utilize 60% solar energy, with plans to reach 80% by 2026.
  • Water conservation: Closed-loop water recycling system that reuses 90% of process water. Our bamboo processing uses 35% less water than conventional pulping.
  • Zero waste manufacturing: All bamboo byproducts are utilized – fiber for products, leaves for compost, and residual biomass for energy generation.
  • Chemical reduction: Our proprietary processing technology uses 70% fewer chemicals than traditional pulping methods. All chemicals used are non-toxic and food-safe.
  • Carbon offset program: We maintain 2,000 hectares of bamboo forest that sequesters more carbon than our manufacturing emits, making us carbon-negative.
  • Local workforce: 85% of our employees come from communities within 30km of our facilities, reducing commuting emissions and supporting local economies.
  • Sustainable packaging: All shipping materials are made from recycled and biodegradable materials, with plastic-free packaging throughout our supply chain.

We are ISO 14001 certified for environmental management and publish an annual sustainability report detailing our environmental impact and ongoing improvement initiatives.

Do you offer carbon footprint information for your products?

Yes, we provide detailed carbon footprint information for all our product lines. Our carbon assessments follow the Greenhouse Gas Protocol and are independently verified by a third-party environmental consultancy.

For each product category, we can provide:

  • Cradle-to-gate carbon footprint (raw material extraction through manufacturing)
  • Cradle-to-customer carbon footprint (including transportation to your location)
  • Full lifecycle assessment (including end-of-life scenarios)
  • Comparative analysis with conventional plastic alternatives

On average, our bamboo fiber products have:

  • 80-90% lower carbon footprint than equivalent polypropylene (PP) products
  • 70-80% lower carbon footprint than equivalent polystyrene (PS) products
  • 50-60% lower carbon footprint than equivalent PET products
  • 30-40% lower carbon footprint than equivalent PLA (corn-based bioplastic) products

For business customers, we can provide carbon footprint certificates for your specific orders to support your sustainability reporting and carbon reduction initiatives.

What is the process for developing custom products?

Our custom product development process follows these key steps:

  1. Consultation: Initial discussion to understand your requirements, including product specifications, intended use, volumes, and timeline.
  2. Concept Design: Our design team creates concept drawings or 3D renderings based on your specifications.
  3. Feedback and Refinement: We collaborate with you to refine the design until it meets your exact requirements.
  4. Prototype Development: We create physical prototypes for your evaluation and testing.
  5. Testing and Validation: Prototypes undergo rigorous testing for functionality, durability, and compliance with relevant standards.
  6. Final Approval: You review and approve the final prototype before production.
  7. Tooling and Setup: We create production molds and set up the manufacturing process.
  8. Sample Production: A small production run to verify quality and consistency.
  9. Mass Production: Full-scale production of your custom order.
  10. Quality Control: Rigorous inspection throughout production to ensure consistent quality.
  11. Delivery: Shipping and logistics coordination for timely delivery.

The typical timeline for custom product development is:

  • Design and prototype phase: 2-4 weeks
  • Tooling and setup: 4-6 weeks
  • Production: 3-4 weeks

Throughout the process, you'll have a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact.

What are the costs associated with custom products?

The costs for custom products include several components:

  • Design fees: Basic design consultation is complimentary. Complex design work may incur a design fee, which is typically credited back on production orders.
  • Tooling costs: One-time fees for creating custom molds:
    • Simple modifications to existing products: $1,000-$3,000
    • New custom shapes/sizes: $3,000-$8,000
    • Complex multi-part products: $8,000-$15,000
  • Sampling costs:
    • 3D printed prototypes: $200-$500
    • Functional prototypes: $500-$1,500
    • Pre-production samples: $1,000-$2,000
  • Unit production costs: Depend on volume, complexity, and specifications. Custom products typically cost 10-30% more than standard products.
  • Minimum order requirements: Custom products generally require higher MOQs (typically 50,000 pieces) to offset tooling and setup costs.

Cost-saving tips:

  • Consider modifying existing products rather than creating entirely new designs
  • Order larger quantities to reduce per-unit costs
  • Choose embossing rather than printed branding for better environmental impact and lower costs
  • Commit to repeat orders to amortize tooling costs over multiple production runs

We provide detailed, transparent quotes that break down all costs involved in your custom project.

Can you match specific dimensions or replicate existing products?

Yes, we can match specific dimensions and replicate existing products with high precision:

  • Dimensional accuracy: Our manufacturing process can achieve tolerances of ±0.5mm, allowing us to match your specific dimensional requirements precisely.
  • Reverse engineering: We can replicate existing plastic or paper products in our eco-friendly bamboo fiber material. Simply provide samples or detailed specifications of the products you wish to replicate.
  • Material thickness: We can produce products with wall thicknesses ranging from 0.8mm to 5mm, depending on the application requirements.
  • Compatibility: We can ensure our products are compatible with existing systems, such as:
    • Matching lids to existing containers

Note that while we can closely match the dimensions and functionality of plastic products, the natural properties of bamboo fiber may require slight design adaptations to ensure optimal performance and durability.
