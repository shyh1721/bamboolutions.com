Bamboo Fiber Processing Plant

Located in Shanwei, Guangdong Province - Operational since 2012

Core of Our Supply Chain

Our Bamboo Fiber Processing Plant in Shanwei serves as the core of our vertically integrated supply chain. Since 2012, we have been dedicated to producing high-quality, eco-friendly bamboo fiber for sustainable packaging solutions.

Supported by 20 square kilometers of self-owned bamboo forests, we ensure a stable and sustainable source of raw material, maintaining complete control over quality and environmental standards throughout the production process.

Our production process is designed for zero solid waste. All by-products are repurposed into liquid fertilizer, significantly reducing resource waste and environmental impact. This closed-loop system underscores our commitment to sustainable, green manufacturing from the ground up.

Patents for processing technology

Advanced Flash-Drying System

Our proprietary flash-drying technology represents a significant advancement in bamboo fiber processing. This system allows us to rapidly dry processed bamboo fiber while maintaining its structural integrity and natural properties.

The system operates at optimal efficiency, reducing energy consumption by 35% compared to conventional drying methods while increasing production capacity. This innovation is protected by multiple patents and exemplifies our commitment to sustainable manufacturing.

  • Energy-efficient operation with 35% reduced consumption
  • Maintains fiber quality and structural integrity
  • Patented technology exclusive to our facility
  • Capacity to process 500 tons of raw material daily

Advanced Processing Equipment

Fiber Extraction System

Our custom-designed fiber extraction system efficiently separates bamboo fibers while preserving their natural properties. This proprietary technology enables us to achieve superior fiber quality with minimal energy consumption.

  • Processing capacity of 25 tons per hour
  • Automated quality control monitoring
  • Water recycling system reduces consumption by 80%

Refinement and Processing Line

Our advanced refinement line transforms raw bamboo fiber into the high-quality material used in our eco-friendly products. The system includes multiple stages of cleaning, refining, and quality control to ensure consistent output.

  • Six-stage refinement process for optimal fiber quality
  • Integrated AI-powered quality inspection system
  • Energy-efficient motors reduce power consumption by 40%
